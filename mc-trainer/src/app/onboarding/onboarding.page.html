Welcome new Student This is your first time in CardLoop! Don't worry, we will show you everything you need! First, you need to find a lesson you are interested in Search for a module And download it Now you can start your journey Search your module in this list and hit play Or you can see details of a specific lesson Simply klick on the lesson for the PopUp and "see Details" After you finished, you get a little feedback of your round GREEN indicates, that you learned a card. If it is “learned”, it will no longer show up in your next test. YELLOW indicates, at how many cards you are getting better in answering. RED indicates, that you need to learn that card more often! If you answered all cards of a lesson correct 6 times in a row, the lesson will be completed! And you also get Achievements for it. Can you find them all? Also if you are interested, you can add your own Lessons! You will find the Button in your List of Lessons In general, you can add a title, description, hashtags and even select a specific color for your personal lesson! Also if you are interested, you can add your own Lessons! Add Questions via the Button On the right side of each answer you can click on the check mark to indicate, that an answer is correct.
